# SAFE-MFI Alignment with the European Union AI Act

## Overview

The European Union AI Act introduces a risk-based framework for regulating AI systems, with enhanced obligations for high-risk applications.

## SAFE-MFI Support

| EU AI Act Requirement   | SAFE-MFI Component      |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Risk Management         | AI Impact Assessment    |
| Data Governance         | Data Assurance Pipeline |
| Technical Documentation | Model Cards             |
| Record Keeping          | Audit Logs              |
| Transparency            | SHAP Explainability     |
| Human Oversight         | Human Review            |
| Accuracy and Robustness | Model Evaluation        |
| Post-market Monitoring  | Drift Monitoring        |

## Operational Benefits

SAFE-MFI enables organizations to:

* document AI decisions;
* support regulatory audits;
* detect model degradation;
* maintain deployment readiness;
* improve governance throughout the AI lifecycle.

## Summary

Although SAFE-MFI was evaluated using credit risk assessment, its governance architecture is applicable to other high-risk AI systems covered by the EU AI Act.

# SAFE-MFI and the OECD AI Principles

## OECD Principles

The OECD AI Principles promote AI systems that are:

* Inclusive
* Human-centred
* Transparent
* Robust
* Secure
* Accountable

## SAFE-MFI Alignment

### Inclusive Growth

Fairness evaluation minimizes unintended discrimination across demographic groups.

### Human-Centred Values

Human oversight enables expert review of automated decisions.

### Transparency

SHAP explanations provide global and local model interpretability.

### Robustness

Cross-validation, performance monitoring, and drift detection improve operational reliability.

### Accountability

Governance artefacts, audit logs, and Model Cards support organizational accountability.

## Summary

SAFE-MFI operationalizes the OECD AI Principles through concrete engineering practices rather than high-level policy statements.
